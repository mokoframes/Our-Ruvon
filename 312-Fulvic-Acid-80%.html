Product Overview

Fulvic Acid is a subclass of humic substances but with higher oxygen content and lower molecular weight. This allows it to pass through micropores in biological membranes (roots and leaves) that Humic Acid cannot. It acts as a "nutrient taxi," picking up minerals from the soil and driving them straight into the plant's vascular system.

  • Universal pH Stability: Unlike Humic Acid (which precipitates in acid), Fulvic Acid remains soluble and active in both acidic and alkaline pH ranges (pH 1 to 14).
  • Potentiator: Enhances the effectiveness of fertilizers and pesticides, allowing farmers to reduce dosage rates.
  • Detoxifier: Helps bind and remove toxins and heavy metals from the soil.

Science: The "Molecular Weight" Magic

Why is it called the "Nutrier Uber"?

The key difference between Humic and Fulvic is Size.

  • Small is Powerful: Humic Acid molecules are large and improve the soil. Fulvic Acid molecules are tiny. This allows them to actually enter the plant through the leaves (stomata) or root hairs easily.
  • Chelation: Fulvic Acid has "Claws" (Carboxyl/Hydroxyl groups) that grab onto nutrients like Iron, Calcium, and Zinc. It wraps them up, protects them from reacting with the soil, and carries them directly into the plant cell. Once inside, it releases the nutrient.

Pro-Tip: The "Potentiator" Effect

Reduce Pesticide Cost by 20%

Because Fulvic Acid enters plant tissues so easily, anything mixed with it also enters easily. If you mix Fulvic Acid with herbicides, fungicides, or insecticides, their uptake increases dramatically.
Recommendation: When tank-mixing with Fulvic Acid, you can often reduce the dose of expensive chemical pesticides by 10-20% while maintaining the same effectiveness.

Application Methods

Foliar Spray

Dose: 0.5 - 1 gm per Liter.

Mix with NPK or micronutrients. It increases the absorption of foliar fertilizers by 30-40%.

Drip Fertigation

Dose: 250 - 500 gm per Acre.

Keeps drippers clean (no sludge) and chelates soil nutrients, making them available to roots.

Hydroponics

Essential Additive:

Since hydroponic water lacks natural humates, adding Fulvic Acid improves nutrient uptake and root health significantly.

Seed Soaking

Soaking seeds in a 0.01% solution accelerates germination and results in vigorous seedlings.
