Zero Tolerance Policy

Quality First

Precision in Every Molecule

In the chemical industry, consistency is not a luxury; it is a necessity. A variance of 0.1% in purity can alter the final product's performance. At Ruvon International, quality assurance is not just a final check; it is embedded in every step of our sourcing process.

We act as the guardian of your product integrity. From auditing the manufacturing plant to inspecting the final container sealing, our technical team ensures that what you ordered is exactly what you receive.

Our Process

The 4-Stage Quality Shield

1. Vendor Audit

Before sourcing, we audit the manufacturer's facility for GMP compliance, production capacity, and raw material storage standards.

2. Pre-Purchase Sample

We draw samples from the specific lot offered. These are tested in our partner NABL labs against the client's TDS.

3. Pre-Shipment Inspection

Final random inspection (FRI) by agencies like SGS/Intertek to verify quantity, packaging, and marking before loading.

4. Container Sealing

Supervision of stuffing to ensure no damage occurs during loading. We maintain a retained sample for 6 months.
