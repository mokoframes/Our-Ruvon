Corporate Overview

Bridging Indian Manufacturing with Global Demand

Ruvon International Private Limited is a registered Merchant Exporter and a dynamic force in the global chemical supply chain. Headquartered in Vadodara, Gujarat, we act as the critical link between India's high-capacity chemical manufacturing sector and international industries seeking reliability, purity, and compliance.

Founded on the principles of transparency and operational excellence, Ruvon International was established to solve a specific problem in the industry: The gap between local production and global expectation. While Indian manufacturers produce world-class chemicals, international buyers often struggle with logistics, documentation, and communication barriers. Ruvon fills this void.

We do not just trade; we manage the entire lifecycle of the productâ€”from sourcing raw materials from GIDC (Gujarat Industrial Development Corporation) certified plants to ensuring the final delivery at foreign ports with complete regulatory adherence.

"Our goal is to make procuring chemicals from India as easy as procuring them locally for our international clients."

How We Work

Our Operational Model

As a Merchant Exporter, we operate on a model of "Asset-Light, Quality-Heavy". We consolidate production from certified manufacturers to deliver efficiency.

Diversified Sourcing

Sourcing from a curated network of manufacturers, allowing us to offer 8 different chemical divisions under one roof.

Independent QC

We deploy independent agencies (SGS/Geo-Chem) for pre-shipment inspection, ensuring uncompromised quality.

Consolidated Logistics

Consolidating shipments from various plants into single containers, reducing logistics costs for our buyers.
