Product Overview

Sodium Bicarbonate is a mild alkaline salt with exceptional buffering capacity. It is non-toxic, biodegradable, and safe for human consumption in its refined form. Ruvon International sources material produced via the carbonation of Soda Ash, ensuring high purity and low heavy metal content.

We offer two distinct grades:

  • Food/Pharma Grade: Meets FCC/USP standards, low lead/arsenic, suitable for baking, antacids, and dialysis.
  • Technical Grade: Cost-effective solution for leather tanning, textile dyeing, and fire extinguishers.

Food Safety Assurance

Our Food Grade Sodium Bicarbonate is manufactured in FSSAI & Halal certified facilities. It undergoes rigorous testing for heavy metals and microbiological contaminants, ensuring it is safe for direct consumption as a leavening agent in bakery products and as an antacid in pharmaceutical formulations.

Industrial Applications

Food & Bakery

Leavening agent (baking soda) for cakes & breads.

Pharma & Health

Active ingredient in antacids & hemodialysis fluids.

Animal Feed

Rumen buffer to improve milk yield in cattle.

Safety

Key component in dry chemical fire extinguishers.

Textile & Leather

pH buffering in tanning and dyeing processes.

Flue Gas Treat

Neutralization of acidic gases (SOx/HCl) in emissions.

Storage Note: Sodium Bicarbonate is stable in dry air but slowly decomposes in moist air. Store in a cool, dry place away from incompatible materials like acids. Shelf life is typically 2 years from manufacture.

Safety Profile

  • Non-Hazardous: Classified as non-dangerous goods for transport.
  • Environment: Biodegradable and eco-friendly.
  • First Aid: In case of contact with eyes, rinse thoroughly with water. Generally safe for skin contact but prolonged exposure may cause irritation.
