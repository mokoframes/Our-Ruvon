Xanthan Gum

High Performance Rheology Modifier

Xanthan Gum is a high-molecular-weight biopolymer produced by microbial fermentation (Xanthomonas campestris). It is famous for its unique ability to increase the viscosity of liquids enormously even at very low concentrations. It is the premier additive for Oil & Gas Drilling Fluids and Food Stabilization.

Product Overview

Xanthan Gum acts as a thickener, stabilizer, and emulsifier. It creates a stable gel structure that prevents ingredients from separating (like oil in salad dressing) or solids from settling (like drill cuttings in an oil well).

  • Salt Tolerance: Maintains high viscosity even in high salinity (brine) environments, crucial for offshore drilling.
  • Thermal Stability: Stable across a wide range of temperatures, from freezing to near boiling.
  • pH Stability: Effective in both acidic and alkaline environments (pH 2 - 12).

The "Shear Thinning" Miracle

[Image of pseudoplastic flow chart]
Pseudoplasticity Explained

Xanthan Gum exhibits extreme Pseudoplasticity (Shear Thinning). This means its viscosity changes based on movement:

  • In Motion (Pumping/Shaking): When force is applied (high shear), it instantly becomes thin and flows like water. This reduces friction in drill pipes or allows ketchup to pour easily.
  • At Rest (Static): As soon as movement stops (low shear), it instantly creates a gel-structure. This suspends heavy drill cuttings in the hole or herbs in a salad dressing preventing them from sinking.

Industrial Applications

Oil & Gas Drilling (Mud)

Primary Viscosifier: Increases mud viscosity to carry cuttings to the surface.
Hole Cleaning: Its gel strength suspends solids when circulation stops, preventing pipe sticking.

Food Industry

Sauces & Dressings: Prevents oil separation and provides "mouthfeel".
Gluten-Free Baking: Replaces gluten to give dough elasticity and structure.

Paints & Coatings

Prevents dripping and splatter during application.

Personal Care

Binder in toothpaste and thickener in lotions.

Household Cleaners

Allows toilet bowl cleaners to cling to vertical surfaces.

Mixing Note: Xanthan Gum loves water too much! If added too fast, it forms "fish eyes" (lumps dry inside, wet outside). Always add slowly to the vortex of a high-speed mixer or pre-mix with oil/sugar.

Safety & Handling Protocols

Safe Biopolymer

Xanthan Gum is non-toxic and biodegradable.

  • Spill Hazard: Extremely slippery when wet. Do not wash spills with water immediately; shovel up dry powder first.
  • Dust: Fine powder can be nuisance dust. Use a mask.
  • Storage: Store in a cool, dry place. Highly hygroscopic.
