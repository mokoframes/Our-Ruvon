Repackaging Services

First Impressions Matter

Packaging is not just about containment; it is about brand identity and safety. Ruvon International provides specialized Contract Packaging (Repacking) services, allowing you to receive chemicals in consumer-ready packs or bulk industrial containers tailored to your needs.

Whether you need 200kg drums repackaged into 1kg bottles for retail, or bulk liquid filled into ISO tanks for efficiency, our facility is equipped to handle hazardous and non-hazardous chemicals with precision.

Private Labeling (OEM)

Want to sell under your own brand name? We offer complete White Labeling solutions. You provide the design; we provide the chemical and packaging.

1. Artwork Submission

Share your brand logo and label design (CDR/AI format).

2. Material Selection

Choose packaging material compatible with the chemical.

3. Printing & Filling

We print high-quality labels and fill the containers.

4. Direct Dispatch

Shipment is sent directly to your client with your branding.

Safety Compliance

We strictly adhere to international packaging norms for Dangerous Goods.

GHS Labeling

All packages carry Globally Harmonized System (GHS) labels with Hazard symbols and safety instructions.

IIP / UN Certification

We use only UN-approved drums and bags certified by the Indian Institute of Packaging (IIP).
