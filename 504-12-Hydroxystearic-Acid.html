12-Hydroxystearic Acid

12-HSA (The Backbone of Grease)

12-Hydroxystearic Acid (12-HSA) is a saturated fatty acid derived from the hydrogenation of Castor Oil. It appears as hard, brittle, white flakes or powder. It is the gold standard hardener and thickener for Lithium Greases, providing superior structural stability, water resistance, and high dropping points compared to standard stearic acid.

Product Overview

12-HSA is produced by the hydrolysis of Hydrogenated Castor Oil. It is essentially Stearic Acid with a Hydroxyl group at the 12th position. This small chemical difference changes everything. It allows 12-HSA to form strong 3D fiber networks in oils, creating high-quality greases that don't melt easily.

  • High Dropping Point: Lithium 12-HSA greases can withstand higher temperatures than simple lithium stearate greases.
  • Oil Bleed Resistance: Holds oil tightly within its structure, preventing separation during storage.
  • Consistency: Ensures smooth texture in cosmetics like lipsticks and deodorants.

Mechanism: The "Grease Sponge"

Why 12-HSA creates better Grease?

Grease is just Oil + Soap (Thickener). The Soap acts like a sponge holding the oil.

  • The Hydroxyl Hook: The unique -OH group on 12-HSA allows molecules to bond strongly with each other (Hydrogen Bonding). When reacted with Lithium Hydroxide, it forms twisted, fibrous soap crystals.
  • The Sponge Effect: These fibers create a robust 3D network (like a sponge) that traps the lubricating oil. This structure is stable under mechanical stress (shear) and heat, which is why Lithium 12-HSA is the world's most popular multipurpose grease.

Industrial Applications

Lithium Greases

The #1 application. Used to make multi-purpose automotive and industrial greases.

Cosmetics

Gelling agent for deodorants, antiperspirants, and lipstick bases. Provides structure and texture.

Paints & Inks

Rheology modifier (thickener) in solvent-based paints to prevent sagging.

Hot Melt Adhesives

Improves setting time and heat resistance in glue sticks and packaging adhesives.

PVC Stabilizers

Used in manufacturing Calcium/Zinc stabilizers for PVC pipes and profiles.

Rubber Processing

Activator and internal lubricant for natural and synthetic rubber compounding.

Handling Caution: 12-HSA is a combustible dust in powder form. Ensure proper grounding to prevent static discharge and dust explosions during hopper filling. Store in a cool place (below 50°C) to prevent caking.
