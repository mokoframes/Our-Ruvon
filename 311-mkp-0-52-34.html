MKP 0-52-34 (The Bloom Booster)

MKP is a fully water-soluble fertilizer containing the highest concentration of Phosphorus (52%) and Potassium (34%) among all fertilizers. Product Overview

MKP (0-52-34) is known as the "Quality Finisher." It is used when the crop needs to stop growing leaves and start filling the fruit. It improves sugar content, fruit color, and weight. Additionally, it has a low salt index (safe for roots) and creates an acidic environment in the water tank, preventing clogging.

  • Disease Control: Foliar application of MKP helps control Powdery Mildew in grapes, apples, and mangoes.
  • Sugar Booster: Potassium helps transport sugars from leaves to the fruit, increasing sweetness (Brix value).
  • Tank Mix Buffer: It acts as a pH buffer, maintaining the acidity of the spray solution around pH 4.5, which increases the efficacy of many pesticides.

Mechanism: "The Nitrogen Brake"

Why Zero Nitrogen?

Nitrogen ($N$) tells the plant to "Grow Green." But during flowering, green growth is the enemy. It eats up the energy needed for flowers.

  • The Stop Signal: By switching to MKP (0% N), you starve the vegetative process. The plant realizes it cannot grow more leaves, so it directs all its energy into the reproductive phase—Flowering and Fruiting.
  • Fungicidal Bonus: MKP makes the leaf surface acidic and strengthens cell walls. This creates a hostile environment for "Powdery Mildew" fungus, acting as a natural disease suppressant.

Application Methods

Drip Fertigation

Dose: 2-3 kg per Acre.

Applied when flower buds start to appear. It cleans drip lines due to its acidic nature.

Foliar Spray

Dose: 5-8 grams per Liter.

Excellent for stopping excessive growth in cotton or vegetables. Can be mixed with most pesticides.
