Product Overview

Seaweed extract works as a "Bio-stimulant." It contains over 60 natural nutrients including carbohydrates, amino acids, and trace elements. It is widely used as a raw material for producing "Zyme" granules, liquid tonics, and organic fertilizers.

  • Stress Buster: Helps plants survive drought, frost, and high salinity (Abiotic Stress).
  • Soil Conditioner: Alginic acid acts as a soil conditioner, improving water holding capacity and soil structure.
  • Nutrient Uptake: Increases the absorption efficiency of other fertilizers (NPK) by chelating micronutrients.

Beyond NPK: The Power of Hormones

Seaweed is not just food; it's a "signal" to the plant to grow better. It contains natural phytohormones:

  • Cytokinins: Promote cell division and delay aging (senescence), keeping leaves greener for longer.
  • Auxins & Gibberellins: Stimulate root elongation and stem growth. A bigger root system means better nutrient uptake.
  • Betaines & Mannitol: Act as "Osmoprotectants." They protect plant cells from bursting or drying out during extreme heat or salty conditions.

Application Methods

Formulation (Raw Material)

Used to manufacture Liquid Bio-Stimulants (10-20% solution) and Zyme Granules (coated on Bentonite/Gypsum).

Foliar Spray

Dose: 0.5 - 1 gm per Liter.

Applied during flowering and fruit setting stages to prevent flower drop.

Drip / Soil Drench

Dose: 250 - 500 gm per Acre.

Enhances soil microbial activity and unlocks fixed phosphorus in the soil.

Seed Treatment

Soaking seeds in a mild seaweed solution accelerates germination and root development.

Turf & Golf Courses

Promotes deep green grass and recovery from foot traffic stress.

Fruit Coating

Used in post-harvest dips to extend the shelf life of fruits.
