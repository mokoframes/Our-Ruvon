Product Overview

SHMP is an inorganic polyphosphate. It acts as a chelating agent that forms soluble complexes with metal ions (Calcium, Magnesium, Iron). This prevents these metals from reacting and forming scale (insoluble deposits) in pipes and boilers. It also acts as a dispersing agent, keeping particles suspended in liquids.

  • Scale Inhibition: Prevents the formation of Calcium Carbonate scale in boiler tubes and cooling towers.
  • Corrosion Control: Forms a thin protective film on metal surfaces, reducing corrosion rates.
  • Deflocculation: Reduces viscosity in ceramic slurries and drilling muds.

Mechanism: The "Chelation Master"

How does it stop Scale?

Scale (Lime) forms when Calcium ions meet Carbonate ions. SHMP prevents this meeting via Sequestration.

  • The Capture: The long phosphate chains of SHMP wrap around the Calcium and Magnesium ions, locking them inside a ring structure (Chelation).
  • Result: Even though the Calcium is still in the water, it is "hidden" inside the SHMP molecule. It cannot react to form scale, and the water behaves as if it is soft. This is called the "Threshold Effect."

Industrial Applications

Water Treatment

Used in industrial boilers, cooling towers, and RO plants to prevent calcium scale buildup and improve efficiency.

Ceramics & Refractories

Super-effective Deflocculant. It reduces the viscosity of clay slurries, allowing for higher solid content with less water.

Textile & Dyeing

Used in scouring and bleaching to sequester hardness salts and iron, preventing stains on fabric.

Food Industry

Used as a preservative, emulsifier, and texture improver in dairy products, canned foods, and meat processing.

Paints & Pigments

Dispersing agent for pigments (like Titanium Dioxide) in water-based paints to prevent clumping.

Detergents

Enhances cleaning power by softening water, allowing surfactants to work better.

Safety & Handling Protocols

Safe Industrial Chemical

SHMP is generally considered safe but requires standard precautions.

  • Dust: Can irritate the nose and throat. Use a dust mask when handling bulk powder.
  • Skin/Eye: May cause mild irritation. Wash affected area with plenty of water.
  • Eco-Friendly: Phosphates can cause eutrophication in water bodies. Dispose of according to local environmental regulations.
