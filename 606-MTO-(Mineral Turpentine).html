Mineral Turpentine Oil

MTO / White Spirit / Mineral Spirits

MTO (Mineral Turpentine Oil) is a clear, water-white petroleum distillate. It is a mixture of paraffins, cycloparaffins, and aromatic hydrocarbons. Known as the "Painter's Best Friend," it acts as the primary thinner for alkyd paints, varnishes, and enamels, ensuring smooth application and brush cleaning.

Product Overview

Ruvon supplies superior quality MTO with controlled aromatic content. Unlike generic thinners which are often adulterated, our White Spirit guarantees a consistent evaporation rate. This ensures that the paint film dries uniformly without forming bubbles or pinholes.

  • Controlled Evaporation: Evaporates slower than Toluene/Xylene, giving the painter more time to work (correcting mistakes).
  • Effective Degreaser: Excellent for removing oil, grease, and grime from metal parts and machinery.
  • Dry Cleaning: Used as a solvent in dry cleaning due to its ability to dissolve stains without damaging delicate fabrics.

Mechanism: "The Wet Edge"

Why use MTO instead of Thinner?

Generic "Thinner" evaporates very fast. MTO is special because of its Slow Evaporation Rate.

  • Flow & Leveling: When you brush paint, it leaves lines. MTO keeps the paint wet for 10-15 minutes, allowing gravity to pull the paint flat and remove brush marks.
  • The Wet Edge: It maintains a "Wet Edge," allowing a painter to paint a large wall section without seeing overlapping lines where the paint dried too quickly.

Industrial Applications

Paints & Varnishes

The #1 solvent for alkyd resin based enamel paints, primers, and wood varnishes.

Dry Cleaning

Used as a solvent to remove oil and grease stains from clothes (Stoddard Solvent).

Metal Degreasing

Used to clean machinery parts, bearings, and tools to remove old grease and oil.

Polishes & Waxes

Solvent for shoe polish, floor wax, and furniture polish. It dissolves the wax for application.

Screen Printing

Used to clean printing screens and rollers after use.

Rust Prevention

Carrier solvent for anti-rust oils and temporary protective coatings.

Static Hazard: Like all petroleum distillates, MTO generates static electricity during pumping. Proper earthing/grounding of tankers and drums is mandatory during transfer to prevent fire.

Safety & Handling Protocols

Flammable & Irritant

MTO is a Class 3 Flammable Liquid (Flash Point > 38°C).

  • Ventilation: Use only in well-ventilated areas. Vapors can cause dizziness.
  • Skin: Repeated exposure causes skin drying and cracking (dermatitis). Use gloves.
  • Storage: Store away from direct sunlight, heat, and open flames.
