Refined Industrial Solvents

Petrochemicals act as the veins of the modern manufacturing sector. From thinning paints to formulating pesticides, high-purity hydrocarbon solvents are indispensable. Ruvon International provides a robust supply chain for these bulk commodities.

We source directly from major refineries (Reliance/IOCL) and chemical complexes, ensuring that our Mixed Xylene, Toluene, and Aromatic Solvents meet stringent distillation ranges. We specialize in bulk tanker handling and drum packaging for safe logistics.
