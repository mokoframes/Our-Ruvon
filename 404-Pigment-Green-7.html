Product Overview

Phthalocyanine Green G (Pigment Green 7) is a synthetic green pigment from the phthalocyanine dye family. It is a soft green powder, which is insoluble in water. It is produced by the chlorination of copper phthalocyanine (Pigment Blue 15:3).

Ruvon International supplies high-purity Pigment Green 7 with excellent dispersion properties. Its outstanding stability against heat, light, solvents, acids, and alkalis makes it suitable for high-end applications like automotive coatings, plastic masterbatches, and outdoor inks.

The "Stability King"

Pigment Green 7 is renowned for its unmatched stability. Its molecular structure makes it impervious to high temperatures (up to 280°C), making it the perfect choice for Plastic Masterbatches that undergo heat extrusion. Furthermore, its exceptional Light and Weather Fastness ensures that exterior paints and automotive coatings retain their vibrant green color for years without fading, even under harsh sunlight and weather conditions.

Safety & Handling Protocols

Industrial Hygiene

Organic pigments are generally safe but dust control is essential.

  • Dust Control: Fine powder can be a nuisance dust. Use local exhaust ventilation.
  • PPE: Wear dust masks (N95), safety goggles, and gloves to prevent inhalation and contact.
  • Spill: Sweep up carefully to avoid raising dust. Dispose of according to local regulations.
