Upstream Solutions

Optimizing Well Performance

Ruvon International supplies high-performance drilling fluid additives and completion chemicals designed for the demanding environments of oil and gas exploration. We understand that wellbore stability and efficient drilling are non-negotiable.

Our portfolio includes viscosifiers, weighting agents, biocides, and shale stabilizers that meet API (American Petroleum Institute) standards. We cater to both onshore and offshore drilling operations, ensuring cost-effective and timely delivery.
