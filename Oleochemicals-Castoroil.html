Natural & Sustainable

The Power of Plant Chemistry

Oleochemicals are chemicals derived from plant and animal fats. They are the sustainable alternative to petrochemicals, playing a critical role in personal care, pharmaceuticals, and industrial lubricants.

Ruvon International specializes in Castor Derivatives (sourced directly from Gujarat, the global hub) and high-quality Surfactants for the Home & Personal Care industry. We bridge the gap between nature's bounty and industrial application.
