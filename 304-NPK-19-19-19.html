Product Overview

Unlike conventional fertilizers (like Urea or DAP) which have low solubility and leave residue, our NPK 19:19:19 is fully soluble. This means it can be absorbed by the plant leaves (stomata) and roots immediately, ensuring zero wastage.

  • Complete Nutrition: Provides N (Greenness), P (Root/Flower), and K (Fruit Quality/Immunity) simultaneously.
  • Non-Clogging: 100% pure, so it won't clog drip irrigation nozzles or spray pumps.
  • Low Salt Index: Safe for young seedlings; does not cause leaf burn when used as directed.

Mechanism: "Law of the Minimum"

Why 19:19:19 is the Best Starter?

According to Liebig's Law of the Minimum, plant growth is dictated not by the total resources available, but by the scarcest resource (the limiting factor).

  • The Bottleneck: Even if you have plenty of Nitrogen, if Phosphorus is low, the plant stops growing.
  • The Solution: NPK 19:19:19 ensures that all three primary limits are removed simultaneously. This provides a "Balanced Diet" during the critical vegetative phase when the plant is building its structure (Leaves, Stems, Roots).

Application Methods

Foliar Spray

Dose: 5-10 grams per Liter of water.

Sprayed on leaves during early morning or late evening. The stomata (leaf pores) absorb nutrients instantly for quick recovery from yellowing.

Drip Fertigation

Dose: 1-3 kg per Acre (depending on crop).

Dissolved in the fertilizer tank and sent directly to the root zone via drip lines. Ensures 90% nutrient use efficiency (NUE).
