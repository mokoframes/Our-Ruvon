Product Overview

Amino Acid Powder 80% is an organic nitrogen fertilizer produced by the hydrolysis of soya protein. It contains a full spectrum of essential amino acids that act as the fundamental building blocks of proteins in plants. Unlike synthetic nitrogen, the organic nitrogen in this powder is readily absorbed and utilized by plants with minimal energy expenditure.

Ruvon International supplies high-purity, chloride-free Amino Acid Powder that is completely water-soluble. It serves as a natural chelator for micronutrients, enhancing their absorption, and acts as a powerful bio-stimulant to help crops recover from biotic and abiotic stress.

The "Protein Builder"

Amino acids are the direct precursors to proteins. When plants are under stress (drought, heat, frost, or pest attack), their ability to synthesize amino acids slows down, halting growth. By applying Amino Acid Powder, you provide "ready-made" building blocks. This bypasses the energy-intensive synthesis process, allowing the plant to save energy for recovery, flowering, and fruit setting. It acts like an "energy booster" for crops, significantly improving yield quality and quantity.

Safety & Handling Protocols

Non-Toxic Organic Product

Derived from natural plant proteins, Amino Acid powder is safe for handling and the environment.

  • Storage: Critical to keep away from moisture to prevent caking. Store in a dry, ventilated warehouse.
  • PPE: Standard dust mask and gloves recommended to avoid inhaling fine powder.
  • Handling: Safe for direct contact, but wash hands after use. Biodegradable and leaves no harmful residue.
