End-to-End Solutions

Delivering Chemistry, On Time & Safely

In the chemical industry, logistics is not just about transportation; it's about handling hazardous materials, managing temperature sensitivity, and ensuring compliance with international maritime laws.

At Ruvon International, we offer a seamless supply chain experience. From the factory floor in Gujarat to your warehouse in New York or Hamburg, we manage every step. Our strategic proximity to Nhava Sheva and Hazira Ports allows us to offer competitive freight rates and faster vessel connectivity.

Logistics Capabilities

How We Move Your Cargo

Ocean Freight

Specialized in FCL (Full Container Load) and LCL shipments. We handle dry vans for packed goods and ISO Tanks for bulk liquid chemicals.

Air Freight

For urgent API samples or high-value low-volume intermediates. We ensure IATA compliant packaging for dangerous goods air transport.

Inland Haulage

Robust trucking network connecting GIDC manufacturing plants directly to the port terminals, ensuring minimal transit time.

Flexible Incoterms

We understand that every buyer has different logistics preferences. We are flexible to work on terms that suit your business model.

Door-to-Door Delivery (DAP/DDP)
Port-to-Port (CIF/CFR)
Ex-Works (EXW/FOB)
FOB
Free On Board

We handle transport, customs, and loading at the Indian port. You manage the ocean freight.

CIF
Cost, Insurance, Freight

We manage everything until the shipment reaches your destination port, including insurance.

EXW
Ex-Works

You pick up the goods directly from our factory warehouse. Maximum control for you.

DAP
Delivered at Place

Hassle-free. We deliver the goods right to your warehouse doorstep.

Hazmat Handling Expertise

Shipping Dangerous Goods (DG) requires specialized knowledge. We are experts in IMDG Code compliance.

  • UN Certified Packaging (Drums/IBCs)
  • Correct DG Labeling & Placarding
  • Hazmat Declarations for Shipping Lines

Documentation Guarantee

Errors in paperwork cause delays. We ensure your clearing agent receives flawless documents.

  • Bill of Lading (BL) & Invoice
  • Certificate of Analysis (COA) & MSDS
  • Certificate of Origin (COO) / GSP
